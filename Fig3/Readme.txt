Monte Carlo simulation data explanation:

dx_den:      Bins of radial density 
dx_com:      Bins of radial center-of-mass distribution
dx_comVx:    Bins of radial center-of-mass vortex distribution
dx_hyp:      Bins of hyperadial distribution
density:     Histogram of density normalized to particle number
com:         Histrogram of center-of-mass distribution normalized to 1
com_vortex:  Histogram of center-of-mass vortex distribution normalized to 1
hyperradius: Histogram of hyperradial distribution normalized to 1
